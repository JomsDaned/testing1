Welcome to our website! This website focuses on the serious problem of animal abuse. We will learn how hurting animals can lead to many issues, not just for them but for everyone. It’s important to know that animals feel pain, too, and they deserve kindness. We want everyone to understand this issue to help stop it. By sharing information and stories, we hope to inspire people to speak up for animals. Together, we can create a world where all animals are safe, healthy, and loved.

Introduction

In this website, we will learn more about animal cruelty. An SDG or sustainable development goals on zero exploitation on animals. Many animals are mistreated by people, and we want to help change that. Some animals are hurt, ignored, or made to do things that aren’t fair. On this website, we want to raise awareness about animal cruelty, determine the causes for this, and teach others why it’s so important to be kind to animals and protect them. Together, we can make sure all animals are treated with care and kindness.


Causes/Reasons for animal cruelty


  • Lack of education about proper care for animals
  • Using animals for entertainment and money
  • No laws about protecting the animals
  • Neglecting the animals

Common Causes

Animal cruelty happens for many reasons. Sometimes, people don’t know they are hurting animals because they don’t understand how to care for them. Other times, people use animals to make money, like breeding them for profit. Animals are also hurt in places where they are used for entertainment, like circuses. Some people just don’t care about animals and treat them badly. Another big problem is that the laws to protect animals aren’t always followed. These are the main reasons why animals are mistreated, and we need to stop it.
